About Us

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering is dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We prepare students to be leaders in the tech world with strong fundamentals and advanced knowledge.

The CSE department at the University of Barishal is highly regarded, dynamic, and forward-thinking. It successfully combines a strong theoretical foundation with practical, hands-on experience. For a student looking for a quality CSE education outside the hustle of Dhaka, it is an excellent and highly competitive choice.

Beyond the classroom, learning is enriched through our state-of-the-art laboratories, a dynamic CSE Society that hosts regular programming contests, workshops, and seminars, and opportunities for innovative project work. Our dedicated faculty members, with qualifications from renowned institutions worldwide, are not only educators but also mentors guiding students towards successful futures.

We take immense pride in our graduates, who are highly sought after and have built rewarding careers in leading software companies, multinational corporations, and academic institutions both at home and abroad.

The Department of CSE at the University of Barishal is more than just an academic unit; it is a community built on a passion for technology, a spirit of collaboration, and a drive to shape the future. We invite you to explore our programs and become a part of our journey.

Research Areas

Artificial Intelligence

BU is advancing AI across multiple fronts: from developing innovative healthcare tools, shaping ethical frameworks for AI in education, to pushing the boundaries of LLM capability and safe AI systems. BU’s approach is both practical and visionary—combining real-world impact with foundational AI research.

Cybersecurity

BU is establishing itself as a leader in cybersecurity research—not only through theoretical and cryptographic advances—but also by addressing real-world threats across systems, hardware, networks, and education. From pioneering detection algorithms to training the next generation of cybersecurity professionals.

Data Science

BU is deeply invested in advancing data science—not just as technological capability, but as an interdisciplinary and socially aware discipline. From infrastructure to faculty leadership, applied research to foundational theory, public health to civic collaboration, BU’s data science ecosystem is both broad and rich in impact.

Robotics

BU offers a great research opportunities in Robotics & Autonomous Systems, combining theoretical instruction with hands-on experience in industry or research. This program is designed for students from engineering or computer science backgrounds, teaching them to synthesize engineering with machine learning .
